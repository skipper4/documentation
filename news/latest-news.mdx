The Skipper Foundation continues to move across Kenya's schools and communities, equipping student leaders, supporting teachers, and speaking life into the next generation. Below are the latest updates from our programs, upcoming opportunities, and a personal reflection from our founder.

This tour represents a significant scale-up of our outreach, bringing leadership training directly to more schools and communities.
