The Skipper Foundation is a faith-driven non-profit organization led by Skipper Nyongesa, a veteran educator with decades of experience shaping young minds across Kenya. Through structured leadership programs, motivational speaking, and hands-on mentorship, the Foundation works to cultivate the next generation of servant-leaders — one school, one student, one community at a time.

## What We Do

The Skipper Foundation operates across four core program areas, each designed to create lasting impact in Kenya's schools and communities:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Student Leadership Training" icon="users" href="/programs/student-leadership">
    Equipping school presidents, prefects, and student councils with skills in communication, decision-making, and servant leadership.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Motivational Speaking" icon="microphone" href="/programs/motivational-speaking">
    Inspiring assemblies, workshops, and conferences that ignite purpose, resilience, and character.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mentorship Programs" icon="handshake" href="/programs/mentorship">

  <Card title="Teacher Development" icon="chalkboard-teacher" href="/programs/teacher-development">
