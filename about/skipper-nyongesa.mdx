Skipper Nyongesa founded The Skipper Foundation after decades of frontline experience shaping young minds as a high school teacher. His journey from the classroom to a national leadership movement is rooted in a single conviction: that leadership is not a title — it is a responsibility to serve others well. With deep classroom wisdom and a faith-anchored purpose, Skipper brings structured, lasting change to schools and communities across Kenya.

## His story

With decades of experience as a veteran high school teacher, Skipper Nyongesa saw firsthand what young people truly needed to thrive. He founded The Skipper Foundation to transform that lifetime of passion into a structured movement — one that reaches students, teachers, and communities far beyond any single classroom.

His approach is rooted in faith, discipline, and the belief that every young person carries untapped leadership potential. The Foundation exists to draw that potential out.

## What he believes

Skipper's philosophy is direct: leadership is not reserved for those with titles or positions. It is a daily choice to serve others well — in the classroom, in the community, and beyond. Every session the Foundation delivers carries this message at its core.
