The Skipper Foundation's Student Leadership Training program is designed specifically for the young people already stepping into leadership roles in Kenya's schools — school presidents, prefects, and student council members. Through full-day workshops and certified training sessions, students gain the practical skills and the character foundation they need to lead their peers with confidence and integrity.

## What students learn

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Communication" icon="comments">
    Students learn to speak clearly, listen actively, and convey ideas with confidence in front of peers and staff.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Decision-making" icon="scale-balanced">
    Structured exercises help students weigh options, think critically, and make sound decisions under pressure.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Servant leadership" icon="hands-helping">
    Students explore what it means to lead by serving — putting the needs of others first while maintaining clear direction.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who this is for

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="School presidents and head prefects" icon="star">
    Students elected to the most visible leadership roles in their school benefit most from the full-day intensive format. The program gives them a shared language and framework they can bring back to their councils immediately.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Prefect bodies and student councils" icon="users">
    The training is built to work with the entire prefect or student council team, not just individual leaders. Group sessions build cohesion, accountability, and a collective leadership culture.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Aspiring student leaders" icon="user-graduate">
    Schools can also open sessions to students who are not yet in formal leadership roles but show potential — giving them the foundation to step up when the opportunity comes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How the program works

<Steps>
  <Step title="School contacts the Foundation">
    A headteacher, deputy principal, or administrator reaches out via the booking form to describe their school's needs and preferred dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Program is tailored to your school">
    The Foundation works with your school to confirm the participant group, session length, and any specific themes relevant to your student body.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Full-day workshop is delivered on site">
    Skipper Nyongesa or a Foundation facilitator delivers the training at your school. Sessions are hands-on, interactive, and grounded in real leadership scenarios.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Students receive certified recognition">
    Participants who complete the training receive certified recognition of their leadership development, giving them a lasting record of their commitment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The Student Leadership Training program is available to secondary schools across Kenya. Sessions can be scheduled during term time or as part of a school leadership retreat.
</Note>
