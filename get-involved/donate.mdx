Every session the Skipper Foundation delivers — every student reached, every teacher equipped, every community visited — is made possible by people who believe in the mission. Your contribution directly funds leadership training, mentorship programmes, and school visits across Kenya. No amount is too small, and every gift moves the work forward.

## Ways to give

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Support a Workshop" icon="chalkboard">
    Fund one full-day leadership training session at a Kenyan high school. Your support puts a trained facilitator in front of hundreds of students for an entire day — equipping them with the skills and mindset to lead.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sponsor a Student" icon="user-graduate">
    Cover the mentorship and materials cost for one student leader for a full year. You become a direct part of that student's journey — their growth, their confidence, and their ability to serve their community.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Partner with Us" icon="handshake">
    Become an organisational partner and help the Skipper Foundation scale its reach across more schools. This giving tier is designed for organisations, corporates, NGOs, and churches that want to make a structural difference.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Your direct impact

When you give to the Skipper Foundation, your contribution goes directly towards:

- **Leadership training** — full-day workshops delivered to students in Kenyan high schools
- **Mentorship programmes** — ongoing, relationship-based mentoring for student leaders
- **School visits** — motivational talks and assemblies that reach entire student bodies

<Note>
  To donate or discuss a giving arrangement, reach out via the [contact page](/get-involved/book-a-session) or email the team directly at [skyjey@gmail.com](mailto:skyjey@gmail.com). The team will guide you through the next steps.
</Note>

## How to get started

You do not need to go through a payment portal to give. Simply reach out to the Skipper Foundation team directly — via email or the contact page — and they will walk you through the process and confirm how your contribution will be applied.

<Tip>
  If you are considering a larger gift or a multi-year commitment, contact the team to discuss a giving arrangement that works for your situation. Every partnership is handled personally.
</Tip>
