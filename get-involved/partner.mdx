The Skipper Foundation cannot reach every school, every community, and every student alone. Partnerships are how the work grows. Whether you represent a school, a church, a corporate organisation, an NGO, or a community group, a partnership with the Skipper Foundation means joining a network of organisations committed to raising a generation of servant leaders across Kenya.

## Who can partner with us

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Schools" icon="school">
    Primary and secondary schools that want to embed leadership culture into their student body — through regular sessions, student leadership clubs, or annual events.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Churches and faith communities" icon="church">
    Faith communities that share the conviction that leadership is a calling to serve. Partner with us to bring structured leadership development to your youth programmes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Community organisations" icon="users">
    Youth groups, community centres, and neighbourhood organisations that want to extend the reach of leadership training beyond the school gate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Corporate sponsors and NGOs" icon="building">
    Companies and non-governmental organisations that want to invest in Kenya's next generation through co-branded events, CSR programmes, and sponsored school visits.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What partners receive

When you partner with the Skipper Foundation, you are not just enabling the work — you become part of it. Partners receive:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Co-branded events">
    Your organisation's name and identity feature alongside the Skipper Foundation at every session, talk, or workshop delivered under the partnership. This includes materials, communications, and on-site recognition.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Priority scheduling">
    Partner organisations receive first access to available dates for sessions and workshops. During peak periods — term openers, national events — partners are prioritised ahead of general requests.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Impact reports">
    Following each session or programme cycle, partners receive a direct impact summary — covering the number of students reached, session outcomes, and the communities served under the partnership.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Why partnerships matter

A single session changes a room. A partnership changes a school. Sustained, structured partnerships are what allow the Skipper Foundation to move from one-off visits to embedded programmes — building leadership culture across an institution year after year.

<Note>
  The Skipper Foundation currently operates across Kenya. Partnerships are open to any school, organisation, or sponsor aligned with the Foundation's mission of faith-driven, servant-oriented leadership development.
</Note>
