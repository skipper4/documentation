The Skipper Foundation delivers leadership training, motivational talks, and community sessions to schools and organisations across Kenya. Whether you want to energise your student body with a motivational assembly or equip your teachers with a professional development workshop, you can request a session directly. The team typically responds within 24 hours.

## Session types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Motivational Talk / Assembly" icon="megaphone">
    A high-energy, faith-rooted session for your full student body. Ideal for term openers, prize-givings, or any moment when your school needs a spark of purpose and direction.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Student Leadership Workshop" icon="users">
    A hands-on, full-day workshop that equips student leaders with practical leadership skills, accountability frameworks, and a servant-leader mindset.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Community Outreach Session" icon="globe">
    Skipper Foundation takes the message beyond school walls — reaching community groups, youth programmes, and neighbourhood organisations across Kenya.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Teacher Development Workshop" icon="book-open">
    A focused professional development session for educators — building tools to identify, nurture, and mentor student leaders from within the classroom.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to book a session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your session type">
    Review the session types above and decide which format best fits your school or organisation's needs. If you are unsure, mention your goals in your message and the team will help you choose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gather your details">
    Before you reach out, have the following ready: your name, email address, phone or WhatsApp number, school or organisation name, estimated number of students, preferred session type, and your preferred dates and location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your request">
    Submit your request using one of the contact methods below. Include as much detail as possible — preferred dates, number of students, and your location in Kenya — so the team can respond with availability quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your booking">
    The Skipper Foundation team will respond within 24 hours to confirm availability, discuss logistics, and finalise the session details with you.
  </Step>
</Steps>
